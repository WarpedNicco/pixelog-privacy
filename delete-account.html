Pixelog – Video Game Tracking App

How to Request Account Deletion

To delete your Pixelog account and all associated data, please follow these steps:

Step 1: Send an email to pixelog.app@gmail.com
Step 2: Use the subject line: "Account Deletion Request"
Step 3: Include your username or the email address associated with your account
Step 4: We will process your request and delete your account within 30 days

What Data Will Be Deleted

When your account is deleted, all of the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Your profile (username, display name, bio, avatar, location, website)
  • Your game entries (ratings, status, playtime, platforms)
  • Your reviews and comments
  • Your game lists
  • Your game diary entries
  • Your likes and follower relationships
  • Your authentication data (email, password)

Data Retention

Once your account is deleted, no data is retained. All personal data and user-generated content is permanently removed from our database. This action cannot be undone.

Aggregated, anonymous data (such as overall game rating averages) may remain, but cannot be traced back to your account.
