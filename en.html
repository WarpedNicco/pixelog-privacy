Privacy Policy

Pixelog – Video Game Tracking App

1. Data Controller

Niccolò Lietha
Email: pixelog.app@gmail.com

2. Data We Collect

When using Pixelog, the following data is collected:

Account data (upon registration):

  • Email address
  • Username
  • Password (stored encrypted)

Profile data (optional):

  • Display name
  • Profile picture
  • Biography
  • Location
  • Website
  • Favorite game

Usage data:

  • Game ratings and status (e.g. "completed", "dropped")
  • Playtime
  • Reviews and comments
  • Game lists
  • Game diary entries (date, platform, notes)
  • Likes and follower relationships

3. Purpose of Data Processing

We use your data exclusively for:

  • Providing and operating the app (account, profile, game tracking)
  • Displaying your activity to other users (if your profile is public)
  • Calculating average ratings and game recommendations

We do not sell your data to third parties and do not use it for advertising.

4. Third-Party Services

Pixelog uses the following external services:

  • Supabase (supabase.com) – Authentication and database. Your data is stored on Supabase servers. Supabase Privacy Policy
  • IGDB / Twitch (igdb.com) – Game information (titles, covers, genres). No personal data is transmitted to IGDB.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored in a PostgreSQL database hosted by Supabase. Access is protected by Row Level Security (RLS), meaning each user can only edit and delete their own data.

Passwords are never stored in plain text – they are stored exclusively as cryptographic hashes.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access – Know what data we store about you
  • Rectification – Correct your stored data
  • Deletion – Delete your account and all associated data
  • Data portability – Export your data

You can edit your profile directly in the app. For complete account deletion or data export, contact us at pixelog.app@gmail.com.

7. Public Content

Reviews, comments, lists, and ratings you create in Pixelog are visible to other users by default. You can set your profile to "private" to restrict visibility.

8. Minimum Age

Using Pixelog requires a minimum age of 13 years. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update this privacy policy as needed. Significant changes will be communicated within the app.

10. Contact

For privacy-related questions, reach us at:
pixelog.app@gmail.com
